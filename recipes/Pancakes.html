Pancakes

The most delicous pancakes! Thin fluffy and light

Before we start make sure you have milk, eggs and flour at home

I suggest eating pancakes with nutella but you can choose whatever topping you want 😀

Ingredients:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 200ml milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • a breeze of salt
  • 200g flour
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  1. stir milk, eggs, sugar, oil and salt into a smooth dough
  2. slowly add the flour while still stiring
  3. now that the dough is finished heat up the pan and apply a bit of oil on the pan!
  4. now let the dough bake from both sides evenly until it gets a nice brown tone and enjoy your tasty pancakes!
